Privacy Policy

Sailor Jupiter Bookkeeping is a private, single-operator application. It accesses QuickBooks Online data (vendors, bills, invoices, chart of accounts, transactions) belonging to the authorized operator's own business for the sole purpose of bookkeeping automation.

Data is processed locally on the operator's computer and is not shared with third parties, sold, or used for advertising.

OAuth refresh tokens are stored in an encrypted local environment file. The App does not collect personal information from any user other than the authorized operator.
